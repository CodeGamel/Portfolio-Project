About me!

Hello! my name is Gamel Collins, a Full-Stack Developer with a passion for technology and coding. I specialize in Front-End Development with backend experience as well. My journey began November of 2023, when I discovered my love for coding, while trying to fix an error in a video game. Since then, I completed a coding bootcamp and continously practice on Codewars. I believe in the power of bettering one-self, which drives me to dive head first into any challenge that comes my way, pay attention to details, and effectively communicate with any working environment I get the pleasure to be around. My goal is to start my career in the tech industry and gather as much information as possible. When I’m not coding, you can find me playing video games, going on hikes or catching a movie in a mall! These experiences often inspire my work and keep me motivated. Feel free to browse my portfolio to see examples of my work, and don’t hesitate to reach out if you’d like to collaborate or learn more about what I do. Let’s create something amazing together! Thanks for visiting!
